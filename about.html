About Brian

An Austin voice actor working in commercial and narration, recording from a dedicated home studio.

Brian is a voice actor based in Austin, Texas. He works mainly in commercial and narration, and he's drawn to the conversational take — the one that sounds less like a performance and more like a real person reading.

He records from a home studio on a Shure SM7B and turns around broadcast-ready files quickly, and he's comfortable taking direction live on a remote session. He voices in English and Albanian.
