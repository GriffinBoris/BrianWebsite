About

The voice behind the reads

Brian is an Austin, Texas-based actor and voice talent drawn to the honest, conversational read — the take that sounds less like a performance and more like someone you'd actually listen to. His work runs from bright commercial spots to steady long-form narration, always aiming for the version that serves the script rather than showing off.

He records from a dedicated home studio on a Shure SM7B and delivers broadcast-ready files with a fast turnaround, and he's comfortable taking direction live over a remote session. He voices in English and Albanian. Whether it's a thirty-second spot or a full explainer, the goal is the same: a natural voice that makes the message land.
