About

A short introduction

Brian is an actor whose work moves easily between the theatre and the camera. Training in classical text and contemporary scene study gave him a precise ear for language and an instinct for the quiet, human moments a scene turns on.

Recent work spans independent film, regional stage, and new-play development. He is drawn to characters caught in change — people trying to be better than the moment allows — and to collaborators who care about the craft. Represented for film, television, and commercial work.
